  • For most small businesses, a combination of content marketing, SEO, social media engagement and online advertising tends to yield the best results. Including social media engagement in your local community, not just with your followers but sharing or commenting on local events can boost your exposure in your local area. As a small business, your goal is to engage the customers that are within a reasonable driving distance to you.
