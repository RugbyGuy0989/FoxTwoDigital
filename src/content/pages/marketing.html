Message Marketing Done Differently

Fox Two Digital - Message Marketing Process

Client Uploads

We take your list of past customers and a promotion from you. Nothing else required from you, which allows you to focus on providing actual services to the ones who reply.

Active Management

We manage your data-base, stopping the messages from going out to past customers who have replied, while we continue to message customers who have no responded.

Re-Usable Framework

Want to run a 4th of July sale, we can manage the client messages. Want to run a similar sale for Labor Day? We keep the frame-work in place, so we can re-run messaging campaigns as often as you want.

End Goal Tracking

We don't just keep track of how many responded to your messages, we track all the way down to who has booked your services. Thats ultimately the number you care about, who ended up a repeat customer.
