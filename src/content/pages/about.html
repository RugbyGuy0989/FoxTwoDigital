About Us

What We Do

Website Developer in Knoxville Tennessee

I was a fighter pilot in the Marine Corps, flying the F/A-18C and D Hornet, then became a flight instructor, teaching fighter fundamentals in the T-45C Goshawk. During my time, I took up HTML and CSS coding to help my parent's small business become more competitive in their local market. I saw that my parents did not possess the technical expertise to create a website themselves and were too busy working to spend the time to learn how.

I also knew that WordPress and similar drag and drop website building programs were bloated, which slowed performance and hurt their search ranking in Google searches. I learned how to optimize the website's speed and performance by building the site for mobile first, then scaling up to larger and more powerful platforms. I’ve taken this approach ever since, creating fast and responsive websites for clients all over the country.

What We Do

Small Business Marketing

As a military spouse, I wanted to find work I found fun and fulfilling, but that could also be done remotely, due to all the moves the military families endure (we just completed our seventh move in 10 years!!) I started with marketing working with boutique firms but saw how quickly a small business's budget was eaten up by an entire team of marketers. This might not matter for large companies who have a $10,000+ per month marketing budget but wasn’t realistic for small businesses.

So, I looked at what worked and what didn’t, and how the principles the large firms employed could be tailored to a smaller budget with a different reach. You don’t need national recognition but want to dominate your city, county, or area code search results. When someone searches “[Your business type] near me, you want to come up in the top 3, if not #1 overall. As a mother, I know that some things are worth paying someone else to do or better left to the experts, and I want to be that marketing expert for you!
